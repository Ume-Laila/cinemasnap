## ✨ What It Does

Browse any movie → CinemaSnap instantly gives you:

- 🔥 Trending, top rated, now playing and upcoming movies
- 🔍 Real-time search by title or genre
- 🎭 Mood Picker — pick your vibe and get matched movies instantly
- 🎬 Full movie detail page with cast, trailers and similar movies
- 🔖 Personal watchlist saved to your browser
- 📱 Fully responsive — works on mobile and desktop

| Layer | Technology |

| Storage | localStorage (Watchlist) |
